## The strategic frame (V2 — sits ABOVE Pylon's infrastructure)

Pylon has mature infrastructure. Rankings run on Sports Thread. Housing runs
through Team Inn. Top-tier sponsors (Jordan Brand, Wilson, Muscle Milk, Battle,
Overtime, BallerTV, Whistle) are already booked with multi-year renewals + category
exclusivity. Registration, scheduling, officiating are built in.

**TOJ does NOT build parallel versions of any of that.** TOJ is the layer above:
production, mid-market sponsor sales, athlete-side pipeline, and everything the
Pylon corp team doesn't have bandwidth to reach one-to-one across 1,261 teams
and ~30,000 active-season athletes.

**Three operational domains define TOJ's mandate:**

1. **SCOUTING** — Digital Scouts crew at events + Subject Report athlete profiles
   at scale + integration with Optimum grading + feeder to Sports Thread rankings.
2. **CREATIVE** — Subject Media production of every team media package, live event
   posting during games, per-athlete hype content, sponsor-integrated content.
3. **EFFICIENCY** — TOJ automation of every sales sequence pre/during/post event,
   CRM nurture, package upsell flows, sponsor fulfillment reporting, merch drop
   ops, City League host toolkit.

Owner's north-star per Kyron: **save Pylon time, produce Pylon revenue.** Every
line item below either saves Pylon labor hours or opens a revenue line Pylon
isn't monetizing today. Not both is not good enough.

## Locked delivery model (customer flow)

### Team-side flow (at registration or in the days after)

1. **Base tournament/season registration** — Pylon corp keeps their existing per-team fee. Untouched.
2. **Team package opt-in** — Recap / Season Pack / Combine Book. Presented at registration confirmation + reinforced by pre-event sequence (see below).
3. **Per-athlete Subscription upsell** — team's head coach can invite the whole roster to a Subject Report subscription checkout page (parents/athletes pay individually).
4. **Optimum Team Analytics add-on** — head coach can add for the season.
5. **Live Event Posting add-on** — teams that want real-time social coverage during their games opt in per event OR bundle in Season Pack.

### Video crew allocation

- Recap tier + Season Pack + Live Event Posting → **Subject Media mentorship crew**
- Combine Book + Digital Scouts + high-value events + international events → **Subject Media contract crew** (event-contracted or year-contracted)

### The three-phase sales sequence (pre / during / post)

Every registered team runs through an automated sequence. Templates get seeded
in the tojcampaign build (see 2g).

**Pre-event (5 touches, T-14 → T-1):**
- T-14: "You're registered for [Event]. Add Team Media Package — early-bird pricing saves $50 vs event-day."
- T-7: "48 hours until packages lock. Combine Book adds every athlete to college-coach outreach."
- T-3: "Final add-on window."
- T-1: "Head coach: get your team's Live Event Posting slot before we max out the Subject Media crew for [Event]."

**During-event (real-time, event-day + event-week):**
- Auto-triggered when a rostered athlete's stat line crosses a threshold: "See [Athlete Name]'s live stats — unlock full Subject Report Season Subscription [$99]."
- Auto-triggered on ranking movement: "[Team Name] just moved to #X in Pylon Power Rankings. Cement your season with the Combine Book."
- Live drop cards ("Athlete of the Half," "Play of the Day") posted to team + parent channels, each with an upsell CTA.

**Post-event (3 touches, T+1 → T+14):**
- T+1: "Here's your team recap reel [preview]. Season Pack unlocks weekly recaps + season book — join before the next event."
- T+7: "Rankings dropped. [Team Name] moved to #X. See the ranking-drop card + your Combine Book preview."
- T+14: "Next event is [Event, Date, Venue]. Registration open. Media package pre-buy is live."

The whole point of the three phases: **teams should feel Pylon around them from
14 days out until 14 days after every event**. Right now they feel Pylon on
game day and nowhere else. That's the time-and-money leak this build closes.
